Biography

Early Life and Education

Born and raised in Spokane, Andy McCune showed entrepreneurial spirit from an early age. Starting with a lemonade stand in kindergarten and progressing to selling energy drinks from his school locker, his business acumen was evident early on. He graduated from Ferris High School in 2014, where he balanced AP classes with various business ventures. After a brief stint at San Diego State University, he left to pursue entrepreneurship in New York.
